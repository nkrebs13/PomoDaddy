# Privacy Policy

PomoDaddy respects your privacy completely.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using SwiftData (backed by Core Data/SQLite). Your Pomodoro session history, statistics, and settings never leave your device.

## Network Requests

PomoDaddy makes **zero** network requests. There is no telemetry, analytics, crash reporting, or any other form of data transmission.

## Third-Party Services

PomoDaddy does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

## Data Location

Your data is stored in the standard macOS application support directory:
```
~/Library/Application Support/PomoDaddy/
```

You can delete this directory at any time to remove all stored data.
